CDC GetTested

This CDC GetTested website helps you find fast and confidential testing locations near you. If you have some of the symptoms of HBV, please use this resource in order to receive HBV tests, as well as tests for other diseases. Just simply enter your zip code, city, or state and a map of locations will pop-up where you can contact the health center by phone or online.

WHO Factsheet on HBV

This factsheet that is distributed by the World Health Organization is filled with useful information on the symptoms, transmission, prevention, and diagnostic information. The factsheet also contains info on the impact of HBV on the world. It is a very useful resource to learn even more about HBV.
